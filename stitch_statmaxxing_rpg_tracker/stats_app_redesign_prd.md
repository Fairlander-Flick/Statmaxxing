# Stats App Redesign PRD

## Core Concept
- **Product**: "Stats" — Personal statistics tracking app for habits and biometrics.
- **Aesthetic**: Premium fitness SaaS (Linear, Notion style) meets RPG progression.

## Visual Identity
- **Themes**: Light and Dark mode variants.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Neutral Base: White/Off-white (Light), #0F0F13 Charcoal (Dark).
  - Primary Accent: Electric Indigo/Violet (#6366F1).
- **Typography**: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans. Bold headings, light body text.
- **UI Elements**: 16px rounded corners, soft shadows, frosted glass effects in dark mode, minimal line icons.
- **Data Viz**: Gradients and glows on chart lines, smooth area charts, circular progress rings.

## Core Screens
1. **Dashboard**: Greeting, "Today's Overview" cards, Weekly hero chart, RPG Hexagon radar chart (centerpiece), Streak counter, Quick-log FAB.
2. **Weight Tracker**: Weight line chart (toggleable periods), current/goal/diff cards, body stats, history list, log input.
3. **Sleep Tracker**: Bar chart duration, quality score gauge, detailed breakdown, average/trend cards, log history.
4. **Nutrition & Water**: Large calorie ring, macro bar segments, water intake tracker (animated fill), meal log, quick actions.
5. **Focus / Productivity**: Focus time display, session history, weekly area chart, Pomodoro timer (secondary), streaks.
6. **Settings**: Profile, preferences (units, notifications, theme), data management.

## Layout Requirements
- **Mobile**: 5-tab bottom bar, floating "+" button, safe area respect.
- **Desktop**: 240px fixed left sidebar, responsive grid (2-3 columns), top header with search/notifications/theme toggle.
